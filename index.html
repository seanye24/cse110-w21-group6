Pomodoro

Work on your tasks!

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Make sure to add tasks that you wish to complete today!
  2. Click “Start” next to the task you would like to start working on/complete
  3. You will work for 25 minute sessions (Pomodoros) with 5 minute break in between.
  4. After 4 Pomodoros, you will be rewarded a longer 15-30 minute break
  5. During each break, you will be asked if you have completed your task. If so, click “yes” and repeat from Step 2 if necessary

NOTE: Within your 25 minute session, press the “End” button if you get distracted or need to leave. This will reset your Pomodoro count toward your long break reward
